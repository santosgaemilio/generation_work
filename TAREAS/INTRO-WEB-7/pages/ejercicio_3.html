Big Picture: Aiming to save the lives of the 500 kids who are dying every day due to road safety issues

Every day, 500 kids die from car accidents in the world. In order to raise global awareness around this issue, the Fédération Internationale de l’Automobile (FIA) asked Luc Besson to direct “Save Kids Lives”, a striking short film destined to be widely spread on the web.
